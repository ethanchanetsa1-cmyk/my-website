THE UNIVERSITY OF ZIMBABWE

Faculty Of Medicine And Health Sciences



Welcome to The University of Zimbabwe's Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences page. We are excited to have you here. Our department brings together science, technology and creativity to improve healthcare in our communities. We are dedicated to innovation in healthcare and research. We aim to train future healthcare workers to tackle healthcare challenges in Zimbabwe and strive to equip students with the knowledge and skills to develop cutting-edge solutions that improve lives in Zimbabwe and beyond.

🏥 Founded with a mission to serve Zimbabwe's healthcare needs, our faculty has produced thousands of skilled medical professionals over the decades.
