Faculty Of Medicine And Health Sciences

About

The Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences produces high calibre, well grounded, competent, patriotic and professional medical doctors and practitioners to meet the healthcare needs of the people of Zimbabwe. It is home to 12 teaching departments that offer a wide variety of educational, research and career options within the context of health care.

The Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences is dedicated to advancing medical knowledge and improving patient care through rigorous research and innovative teaching. Our faculty members are not only accomplished educators but also leading researchers in their fields. They are committed to fostering an environment that encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and a strong ethical foundation in all aspects of healthcare.

In addition to traditional classroom learning, the faculty emphasizes hands-on experience through clinical rotations and internships. Students have the opportunity to work in diverse healthcare settings, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world situations. This experiential learning approach equips graduates with the practical skills needed to navigate the complexities of modern medicine and patient care.

The Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences also places a strong emphasis on community engagement and service. We believe that healthcare extends beyond the confines of hospitals and clinics, and our students are encouraged to participate in outreach programs that address public health issues. By working closely with the community, students gain valuable insights into the social determinants of health and the importance of preventive care.

The path of the healthcare professional is not an easy one but it is enormously rewarding – from bringing new lives into the world, to rehabilitating accident victims and performing lifesaving surgery. It takes hard work and dedication to become a competent pharmacist, physiotherapist, occupational therapist, nurse, dentist, medical laboratory scientist, biomedical engineer, radiographer or doctor. As an institute of higher learning, the Faculty takes academic excellence as the hallmark of its existence by promotion and enhancement of science through relevant research.

Looking ahead, the Faculty is committed to embracing emerging technologies and integrating them into the curriculum. With advancements in telemedicine, artificial intelligence, and personalized medicine, the healthcare landscape is rapidly evolving. Our faculty is dedicated to preparing students for these changes, ensuring they are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in a dynamic healthcare environment.
